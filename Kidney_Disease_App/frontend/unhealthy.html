Important: Based on the health parameters provided, our analysis indicates potential signs of Chronic Kidney Disease (CKD).

Please consult with a healthcare professional immediately for proper diagnosis and treatment.

🚨 Immediate Actions Required

  • ❗ Consult a Nephrologist: Schedule an appointment with a kidney specialist as soon as possible
  • ❗ Complete Medical Tests: Get comprehensive kidney function tests (GFR, creatinine clearance)
  • ❗ Monitor Symptoms: Watch for swelling, fatigue, decreased urination, or nausea
  • ❗ Blood Pressure Control: Monitor and manage blood pressure daily
  • ❗ Medication Review: Discuss all current medications with your doctor

📋 Lifestyle Recommendations

🥗

Kidney-Friendly Diet

  • Limit protein intake
  • Reduce sodium (less than 2g/day)
  • Control potassium and phosphorus
  • Avoid processed foods
💊

Medication Management

  • Take prescribed medications regularly
  • Avoid NSAIDs (ibuprofen, aspirin)
  • Discuss supplements with doctor
  • Monitor for side effects
💧

Fluid Management

  • Monitor fluid intake
  • Avoid excessive water consumption
  • Track daily urine output
  • Limit alcohol intake
🩺

Regular Monitoring

  • Monthly blood tests
  • Regular blood pressure checks
  • Track weight daily
  • Monitor for swelling (edema)

⚡ Warning Signs to Watch For

Severe Symptoms (Emergency)
  • Severe swelling in legs/feet
  • Difficulty breathing
  • Chest pain
  • Confusion or drowsiness
Monitor These Symptoms
  • Decreased urine output
  • Persistent fatigue
  • Nausea and vomiting
  • Loss of appetite
